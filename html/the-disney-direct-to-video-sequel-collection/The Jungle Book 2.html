THE JUNGLE BOOK 2 OFFICIAL TRAILER



SYNOPSIS

Two years after the events of The Jungle Book, Mowgli is having doubts about staying in the man village and wants to go back to the jungle. While his new adoptive family wants for him to be safe, he still wants to go back to where he grew up at. He then embarks on another adventure in the jungle that gets his friends in the jungle and in the man village worried, while he hates the feeling of have to choose between the two.
