TARZAN II OFFICIAL TRAILER



SYNOPSIS

During the Son of Man montage from the orignal Tarzan , this midquel shows Tarzan as a young boy who wants to find his place in his home in the jungle and amongst his gorilla family. But when a storm separates him from his family, he decides to take this opportunity to find his identity of who he is and how he can use this adventure to strengthen his character to become the man he will become.
